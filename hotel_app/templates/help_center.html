Pending & On Hold

Understanding booking and payment statuses

Status Explanations

Pending Status

Your booking or transaction is being processed. This typically takes 2-24 hours depending on verification requirements and hotel confirmation.

On Hold Status

Your request requires additional verification or manual review. This may happen for high-value transactions, first-time bookings, or when additional documentation is needed.

Note: You'll receive email notifications for all status changes.
