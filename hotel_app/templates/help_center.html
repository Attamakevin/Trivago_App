Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a booking through Trivago?

To make a booking through Trivago: 1) Search for your destination and dates, 2) Compare prices from different booking sites, 3) Click on your preferred option to be redirected to the booking site, 4) Complete your reservation on the partner site. Trivago is a comparison platform that helps you find the best deals.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Cancellation and modification policies depend on the booking site and hotel you booked with. You'll need to contact the booking site directly or check your booking confirmation email for specific terms. Trivago can help you find the contact information for your booking site.

Why do I see different prices on different sites?

Prices can vary between booking sites due to different deals, promotions, or partnerships they have with hotels. Some sites may include taxes and fees in the displayed price, while others add them later. Trivago shows you these differences so you can make an informed choice.

Is Trivago free to use?

Yes, Trivago is completely free for users. We make money through commissions from our partner booking sites when you complete a reservation. There are no hidden fees or charges for using our comparison service.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact us through multiple channels: 1) Live chat (available 24/7), 2) Email support@trivago.com, 3) Phone +1-800-TRIVAGO, or 4) Through our mobile app. For booking-related issues, you may need to contact the booking site directly.

What should I do if I have a problem with my hotel stay?

First, try to resolve the issue directly with the hotel. If that doesn't work, contact the booking site you used to make the reservation. They are responsible for handling booking-related issues. You can also contact Trivago support for assistance in finding the right contact information.
