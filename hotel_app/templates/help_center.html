Deposit

How to add funds to your account

How Deposits Work

Deposit can be done on the "Account" Page. Click on the "Deposit" button to redirect to the online deposit customer service to assist in remittance Deposit.

After transferring the money according to the account provided by the platform's customer service, be sure to submit a screenshot of the successful transfer to the account. Please make sure that the name of the person you are transferring to and the amount you are transferring are the same as the one being provided.

If you encounter any unsolvable problems during the Deposit process, please contact the on-duty online customer service.

Due to a large amount of information, please make sure to check the Deposit account number of this platform carefully before making Deposit. The platform occasionally changes the Deposit account number. If you have any inquiries, please contact the platform's online customer service.

Please transfer with your bank account, the platform Deposit only allows the owner of the account to transfer money, other people or anonymous 's transfers will affect the security of the account.

Withdraw

How to withdraw funds from your account

Withdrawal Process

After completing every set of reservation, you may submit a withdrawal. Please bind your bank information on the platform before you submit for withdrawal.

Click the "Withdrawal" button after entering the amount you want to withdraw and enter your withdrawal password to withdraw. The specific arrival time is subject to the bank's arrival time.

Withdrawal charges are subjected to the amount and can only be made after have completed the number of reservation assigned by the platform.

Note: Withdraw time is 10:00 AM to 22:00 PM

Pending & On Hold

Understanding booking and payment statuses

Status Explanations

Reservations that is not submitted after receiving an order will be in "Processing". All you need to do is click the "Order" button to complete the outstanding order. Luxury Reservations

Premium booking service for exclusive properties

Luxury Booking Experience

Our luxury reservation service provides access to the world's most exclusive hotels, resorts, and private properties with white-glove concierge service.

The merchants want to increase the sales of their hotel reservation and also to attract more agents to help increase the turnover of their hotel, which would help them to increase their economic impact and visibility, so they created Luxury Reservation

What is Luxury Reservation?
Deluxe Reservation only exists in 0-3 of the 35 hotel reservations and agents will be randomly assigned 0-3 Luxury Reservation. However, to be fair, agents will receive multiple random commission. The Luxury Reservation are randomly assigned by the system and as the commission is multiple random, the chances of being assigned are not very high. Your account balance will be in a frozen state when you get a Luxury Reservation and you will need to contact with on duty customer service

What is Special Reservation?
The Special Reservation is a benefit given to agents by the merchants, the chances of getting it are very low, in order to promote high sales and high views so that agents get multiple random commission.
